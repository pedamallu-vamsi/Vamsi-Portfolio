About Me

Hello World! My name is Vamsi. I was born in Bhimavaram, a small town in the West Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh.
Because of my dad’s profession, I moved cities often during my childhood, but we eventually settled in Vijayawada, where
I have lived for over 10 years.

I’m passionate about sports such as Taekwondo, Swimming, Athletics, Cricket, Badminton, and more. I also enjoy watching
movies and series in multiple languages including Telugu, Hindi, English, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese.

I completed most of my schooling in Vijayawada and actively participated in Taekwondo competitions. I am a National Taekwondo Player, a 7-time District Gold Medalist, and have played the Zonal Championships twice, along with many other
achievements (which you can see here).

I completed my Intermediate at Narayana Junior College, Vijayawada. I qualified both JEE Mains and JEE Advanced, and secured
a rank of 1866 in AP EAMCET. I am currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering at the
Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam through my JEE Mains rank.

I take a strong interest in Data Structures & Algorithms, Competitive Programming, and Web Development. I regularly solve problems on LeetCode, where I have solved nearly 100 questions and maintain an 85-day streak. You can check my LeetCode profile here. I also actively participate in coding contests that push me to think faster and improve consistently, and challenge myself.

As I continue learning and building new projects, I aim to grow into a skilled engineer who can solve real-world problems with creativity and precision. This portfolio represents where I am today, and the journey I’m excited to continue.
